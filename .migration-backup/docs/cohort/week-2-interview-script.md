# Week 2 — Problem Discovery Interview Script

**Product:** Sweet Tooth — AI front desk for Pakistan home bakers (WhatsApp + marketplace)  
**Cohort idea:** #8 Social-Commerce Support Agent (+ #1 WhatsApp Business Brain)  
**Goal:** Confirm the wedge before building more features. **5 interviews minimum.**

---

## Who to interview

Pick **one** sharp segment:

- Home baker in **Lahore Gulberg** or **Islamabad F-7** doing 5+ orders/week  
- Baker who sells mainly via **Instagram DMs + WhatsApp** (not a shop)  
- Baker overwhelmed by **same questions after 9pm**

**Where to find them:** HomeBakersPK listings, Instagram `#homebakerlahore`, university bake-sale groups, family/friends, local Facebook groups.

---

## Rules (Mom Test)

- Ask about **their life and workflow today** — not “would you use our app?”  
- Ask for **specific past events** — “last time someone asked price at midnight, what happened?”  
- **Silence is fine** — let them talk  
- **Don't pitch** until the last 2 minutes  

---

## Opening (2 min)

> Assalam-o-Alaikum, I'm [name] from [squad]. We're researching how home bakers handle customer messages and orders — not selling anything today. Can I ask you about your last week of customer chats? It'll take about 20 minutes.

---

## Core questions

### 1. Their workflow today
1. Walk me through how a **new customer** finds you and places an order today.  
2. What apps do you use every day? (WhatsApp, Instagram, Cash, notebook?)  
3. How many **customer messages** do you get per day? Who replies?

### 2. The painful moment (most important)
4. Tell me about the **last time** you lost a sale or annoyed a customer because you replied late.  
5. What questions do you answer **over and over**? Can you show me your WhatsApp? (count repeats)  
6. What happens when you're **busy baking** or it's **late at night**?  
7. How do you share **prices** today? Ever sent the wrong price by mistake?

### 3. Money and trust
8. How do customers pay? (COD, advance, bank?) What goes wrong?  
9. Eggless, delivery area, custom messages — how do you handle special requests?  
10. What would make you **trust** an auto-reply bot with your customers?

### 4. Alternatives
11. Have you tried HomeBakersPK, Instagram orders only, or a WhatsApp catalog link? What worked / didn't?  
12. What do you use instead of hiring help?

### 5. Close (2 min) — only now mention product
> We're building a tool that answers customer questions from **your real menu** on WhatsApp and logs orders. Would you try it free for 2 weeks if we set it up for you?

- Would they give WhatsApp access for a pilot?  
- Can they introduce 2 other bakers?

## Signals that validate the wedge

**Strong:** “I missed 3 orders last Eid because I was in the kitchen” / shows chat with 10× “price?”  
**Weak:** “Sounds cool” / no specific story / doesn’t use WhatsApp for sales  

**Pivot if:** They only sell via one fixed menu link and get &lt;2 msgs/day — find a busier baker.

---

## After 5 interviews — squad debrief

1. What were the **same 3 problems** in every conversation?  
2. Did anyone **already pay** for a workaround (VA, second phone)?  
3. Narrow wedge one sentence: “We help [who] with [pain] via [channel].”  
4. Update README **Problem** section with real quotes (anonymous if needed).
